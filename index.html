About

Welcome to my website! I am a PhD candidate in the Economics Department at the University of Toronto.

My research interests are in labor economics, applied microeconomics, and the economics of immigration. I study earnings inequality between immigrants and non-immigrants, the effects of immigration on workers and firms, and imperfect competition in the labor market.

I am on the 2024-2025 job market.

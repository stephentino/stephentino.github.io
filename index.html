About

Welcome to my website! I am a PhD candidate in the economics department at the University of Toronto.

My research interests are in labor economics, applied microeconomics, and the economics of immigration. My work focuses on understanding the sources of earnings inequality between immigrants and non-immigrants and the effects of immigration on workers and firms.

I will be on the 2024-2025 job market.

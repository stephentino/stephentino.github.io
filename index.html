About

I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at Toronto Metropolitan University (TMU). As a labour and immigration economist, I study the immigrant pay gap and how immigration affects the job market.

I received my PhD in Economics from the University of Toronto in 2025. I also hold an MA in Economics from the University of Michigan, a BSc in Financial Economics from the University of Toronto, and a BA in Criminology from TMU.
